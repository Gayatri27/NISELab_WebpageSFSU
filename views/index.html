Networking, Information Security, and Education (NISE) Laboratory

About

The Networking, Information Security and Education Laboratory (NISE) was established in 2016 and it is now located at SCI383 in San Francisco State University. The research of NISE Lab focuses on :

  • Building new protocols and architectures to enhance the performance and security of computer networks and information systems.
  • Building new infrastructures and programs to broaden participation in computing and computer science.
